Objectives

Part 1: Connect a Home Gateway to the Network

Background / Scenario

In this activity, you will add a home gateway and several IoT devices to an existing home network and monitor those devices through the home gateway.

Note: This activity is scored. Be sure you change the Display Name when directed to do so or the scoring will not be accurate. Click Check Results at any time to view your progress.

Instructions

Part 1: Connect a Home Gateway to the Network

Step 1: Add a Home Gateway.

a. In the Device-Type Selection box, click Network Devices, and then Wireless Devices.

b. Click Home Gateway, and then click in the Logical workspace to add the device.

c. Click Home Gateway0, then the Config tab. Change the Display Name to Home Gateway.

Step 2: Connect the Home Gateway to the cable modem.

a. Choose Connections > Copper Straight-Through.

b. Click the Cable Modem and connect one end to Port 1.

c. Click the Home Gateway and connect the other end to the Internet port.

Part 2: Add End User Devices to the Network

Step 1: Add a wireless tablet to the workspace.

a. Click End Devices > Tablet. Add it.

b. Rename Tablet PC0 to Tablet (Config tab).

Step 2: Connect the tablet to the Home Gateway network.

Tablet is already connected to cellular (blue line to cloud).

a. In Config > Wireless0.

b. Change SSID from Default to HomeGateway. Wait (or Fast Forward Time) for 192.168.25.x address.

c. Tablet now shows cellular and Wi‚ÄëFi links.

Step 3: Access the home gateway from the tablet.

Tablet > Desktop > IoT Monitor, login (admin/admin). No devices yet.

Part 3: Connect IoT Devices to the Network

Add three IoT devices and register them.

Step 1: Add IoT device to the wired network.

Add and configure a Lamp.

a. End Devices > Home > Lamp.

b. Open Lamp, click Advanced.

c. I/O Config: change adapter to PT-IOT-NM-1CFE.

d. Config: rename to Lamp.

e. FastEthernet0: set DHCP.

f. Cable Lamp FastEthernet0 to available Home Gateway Ethernet (Copper Straight-Thru).

Step 2: Add IoT devices to HomeGateway wireless network.

Add Fan and Door (wireless).

a. End Devices > Home: add Fan and Door.

b. Rename Fan to Fan.

c. Rename Door to Door.

d. Wireless0 of each: SSID already HomeGateway; wait for DHCP (Fast Forward Time if needed).

Step 3: Configure IoT devices to register with the Home Gateway server.

For each device: Config > Settings > IoT Server: select Home Gateway.

Step 4: Verify registration.

Tablet > IoT Monitor > Login. All three should appear; expand and control them. May take a minute.

Part 4: Add Bluetooth Devices

Add a Bluetooth Speaker and Music Player; pair them.

Step 1: Add a Bluetooth speaker.

a. End Devices > Home > Bluetooth Speaker.

b. Auto-connects; wait for DHCP.

c. Rename to Speaker.

d. Config > Bluetooth: Port Status On.

Step 2: Add a portable music player.

a. End Devices > Home > Portable Music Player.

b. Wait for DHCP.

c. Rename to Music Player.

Step 3: Pair the Music Player to Speaker.

a. Enable Bluetooth Port Status.

b. Discover > select Speaker > Pair > Yes.

c. Alt + click Music Player to interact.

Question:

What happens?

Answer Area

The screen of the Music Player lights up and the Speaker is playing sound.

Step 4: Explore the network.

Add more devices; Alt + click to control (Music Player, Door, Lamp, Fan). You can also manage IoT devices from IoT Monitor on Smartphone or Tablet.
